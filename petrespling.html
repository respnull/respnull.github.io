Pet Respling

Pet Respling allows you to get a respling as a desktop pet on your PC!

He moves around, gets hungry, thirsty and sleepy. If you don't want to babysit him, you can turn any of them off!

You can additionally change his color scheme, modify his size, toggle gravity and do more!

I might work on an android version of this project, who knows?
